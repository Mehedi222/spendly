Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details such as your name and email address.
  • Expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic usage information to help us improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not track you across third-party websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • Respond to support requests or feedback you send us.

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your financial information stays private.

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and accessed only as needed to deliver the functionality of the app. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Access and review all expense data you have entered at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Export your data before deletion.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our site.
